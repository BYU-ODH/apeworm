BYU's VowelWorm

VowelWorm will show you where in your mouth your tongue is compared to where the vowel you're trying hit is.

Note: The vowel space is actually much smaller in the mouth than represented here by the trapezoid.

Press the mic button and try to get the dot to hover around the symbol of your target vowel.

The VowelWorm can plot vowel features on a canvas in real-time. It is based on the work done at the Department of Computational Perception at Johannes Kepler Universit√§t Linz.
